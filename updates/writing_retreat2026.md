From March 9 to March 12, the lab stepped away from out usual routines to the tranquil woods and lakes of Siegsdorf for a dedicated DFG grant writing retreat.
Escaping the daily routine allowed us to fully immerse ourselves in drafting proposals and brainstorming future projects.
While we worked incredibly hard, we also had an absolute blast!
The peaceful, distraction-free setting was the perfect backdrop for productive writing sessions, sharing ideas, and enjoying some well-deserved team binding out in the nature.
